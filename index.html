~# Hak4Kidz Overview

Hak4Kidz is the first official youth based ethical hacking conference in Chicago that is *not* a hackathon.

~# 2017 Chicago Keynote Announced!

This year we are excited to announce that Jeff Man will be our keynote speaker!

Jeff is a respected Information Security expert, advisor, speaker, teacher, advocate, and curmudgeon. He has over 33 years of experience working in all aspects of computer, network, and information security. He has held security positions with NSA (National Security Agency), the DoD (Department of Defense) and private-sector enterprises. A little known fact, Jeff Man was part of the first penetration testing "red team" at NSA.


We are excited to have him share his experiences protecting the United States of America with us on June 3rd in Chicago.
