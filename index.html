About Hak4Kidz

Hak4Kidz is the first official youth based ethical hacking conference in Chicago that is *not* a hackathon.

The mission is to educate young minds on the importance of Internet safety, cyber-security, anti-cyberbullying, and STEHM (STEM with an ethical Hacking mindset).

ak4Kidz is dedicated to educating youths on cyber-security, along with the communical benefits of conferences and ethical hacking.
